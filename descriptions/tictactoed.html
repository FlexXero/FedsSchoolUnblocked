Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe, also known as Noughts and Crosses, is a classic two-player game that is simple yet strategically rich. It is traditionally played on a 3x3 grid, with players taking turns to mark spaces in the grid with their respective symbols: 'X' and 'O'. The goal is to be the first player to get three of their symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
